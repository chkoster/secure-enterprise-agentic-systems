## Strategic Architecture Lens

Every chapter and pattern should be tested against:

1. Does this strengthen the reader's ability to make better decisions?
2. Does this hold up under executive and board scrutiny?
3. Is this durable beyond current tools and vendors?
4. Does this reduce ambiguity for practitioners?
5. Does this connect security architecture to business outcomes?
